Overview

Short description of the project

High-Level Purpose

The purpose of this game will be to empower middle and high school students to mitigate algorithmic harms.

Audience & Context

Middle/high school students who are casual gamers and who use social media or other algorithmic systems often. Players have interacted with algorithmic systems before, but are unsure about how algorithmic bias can cause real-world harm or believe that it is not possible/necessary for them to anything about algorithmic bias.

Barriers

  1. Motivation/relevance: it can be hard to directly see the impacts of algorithmic bias as a user.
  1. Unfamiliarity/complexity: players may not understand how algorithmic systems work and thus be intimidated by the complexity of the systems.
